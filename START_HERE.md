# 🚀 START HERE - Ilm Dost Setup

**Welcome to Ilm Dost** (Your Trusted Learning Companion)!

## ⚠️ CRITICAL FIRST STEP

**Before you can use the app, you MUST deploy the backend!**

### 🔧 Deploy Backend NOW

1. **Open Make Settings**
   - Click the ⚙️ Settings icon
   - Or find "Settings" in the menu

2. **Find Supabase Section**
   - Look for "Supabase" or "Backend"

3. **Click Deploy Button**
   - Button says "Deploy Supabase Function" or "Deploy Backend"
   - Click it and wait

4. **Wait 60 Seconds**
   - Watch for "✅ Deployment Successful"
   - Do NOT close the page

5. **Refresh Your App**
   - Press Ctrl+Shift+R (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+R (Mac)
   - Wait 10 seconds

**Done? Great! Now continue below...**

## 🎓 Quick Start (After Deploying Backend)

### Option 1: Demo Account (Fastest!)

1. Look for the login screen
2. Click **"🎓 Try Demo Account"** button
3. You're in! Explore the platform immediately

### Option 2: Create Your Account

1. Click **"Sign Up"** tab
2. Fill in:
   - **Name**: Your full name
   - **Email**: your@email.com
   - **Password**: At least 6 characters
3. Click **"Sign Up"**
4. You'll be auto-logged in!

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### "Email rate limit exceeded"
✅ **Solution**: Use the **"Try Demo Account"** button instead!

### "Sign up not working"
✅ **Solution**: Make sure you deployed the backend first (see top of this page)

### "Data not saving"
✅ **Solution**: 
1. Deploy backend from settings
2. Hard refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R)
3. Try demo account

### "Can't login"
✅ **Solution**:
1. Check backend is deployed
2. Wait 2 minutes after deployment
3. Use demo account to verify it works
